About

Jim is a recent (2020) transplant to the greater Boston area. Originally from northeastern Ohio, he graduated with a bachelor of science in Computer Information Systems from Youngstown State University in December of 2019.

Beginning in December 2020, Jim has been a system integration SQA (Software Quality Assurance) engineer with Sensitech, a local pharmaceutical and food supply chain partner that produces temperature and humidity monitoring solutions for more than 16 million shipments annually.

Jim’s integration role has given him a solid foundation of the Agile SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle). In the following years, his role has expanded into several other internal software projects SQA testing, as well as leading the team scrums, managing the team’s Jira board, and managing the department’s interns and their projects.
